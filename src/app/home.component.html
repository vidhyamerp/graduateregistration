INSTRUCTIONS FOR ONLINE GRADUATES REGISTRATION
  • Kindly read the instructions carefully before you start filling application form.
  • Graduates residing in the Revenue Districts of Coimbatore, Erode, The Nilgiris and Tirupur and who are willing to enroll themselves in the respective Register of Graduates of the Districts maintained by this University may apply using this registration form.
  • A person is eligible for registration only after a period of three years of obtaining degree of any recognized university in the territory of India.
  • The candidate should posses the registration fee of Rs. 25/- (Rupees Twenty Five Only) online to The Registrar, Bharathiar University.
  • The Candidate should upload the following documents
    • Self attested copy of aadhar card
    • Copy of the degree certificate or provisional certificate have to be duly attested by a Gazetted officer, Syndicate / Senate member of Bharathiar University in proof of qualification.
    • Residence Certificate (download residenece certificate)
    • Demand Draft copy
    • Signature & Photo (size below 20KB)
